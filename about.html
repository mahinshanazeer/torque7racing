Our Founding Members' Vision

We are a group of seven individuals who have embarked on a collective journey through life. United by a shared passion for RC cars and mountain biking, our story began in 2017, a time when radio-operated auto models were still a novelty in Kerala. At that point, only a handful were acquainted with these pursuits. As time progressed, so did our camaraderie, culminating in the formation of a community in 2018. Our mutual devotion to RC, mountain biking, and motorcycling served as the cornerstone of our bond.

Our Journey: From Passion to Purpose

The beginning of Team Torque-7 Racing dates back to 2018 when one of our founding members, a curious teenager from Kochi, stumbled upon videos by RCspark Studio on YouTube. Fascinated by the intricate world of RC cars, he delved deep into learning about brands, electronics, and mechanics, gaining knowledge through relentless online research. His enthusiasm led him to connect with local RC car enthusiasts, eventually forming a community named Team RC Kerala.

Our first landmark event was in 2019, with Kerala's maiden hobby-grade RC car racing held at Rajagiri College of Engineering. It marked the start of an incredible journey. Later, we hosted our first collaborative show at Nehru College of Engineering, Palakkadu. Initially, we operated under the name TRF Racing (Team Radio Force), but we rebranded ourselves as Torque-7 Racing to reflect our passion for high-torque electric motors and the significance of our seven founding members.

What started as a hobbyist community gradually evolved into a vibrant platform for exploration and innovation. Alongside RC car events, we expanded into mountain biking, organizing thrilling competitions in partnership with renowned brands like Decathlon India and Polygon Bikes India. From humble beginnings with just one mountain bike and ten RC cars, we now boast one of the largest collections of RC cars in India. Our proudest moment was representing Kerala at the National RC Car Championship in Mysore, Karnataka, where we clinched multiple prizes.

Embracing the brevity of our existence on this planet, we find the essence of living in savoring every fleeting moment.
