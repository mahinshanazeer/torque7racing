About Us

Discover Happiness by Living the Dream

Trailblazing Adventures: Inspiring Kerala's Youth Through RC, Mountain Biking, and Motorcycling – Promoting a Drug-Free, Healthy, and Happy Generation.

Our Founding Members' Vision

We are a group of seven individuals who have embarked on a collective journey through life. United by a shared passion for RC cars and mountain biking, our story began in 2017, a time when radio-operated auto models were still a novelty in Kerala. At that point, only a handful were acquainted with these pursuits. As time progressed, so did our camaraderie, culminating in the formation of a community in 2018. Our mutual devotion to RC, mountain biking, and motorcycling served as the cornerstone of our bond.

Polygon Game of Trails – Chapter 2: Promoting Mountain Biking Culture in Ernakulam

In 2021, we proudly hosted the Polygon Game of Trails – Chapter 2 in Ernakulam, in collaboration with Old Spoke House, Aluva. Held on our home track in Aluva, this event aimed to promote cycling culture in India and educate enthusiasts about mountain biking and off-road riding, making this event a milestone in fostering a passion for MTB and ATB adventures.
