RC Models Expo and shows

Enthusiastically organizing RC car expos and captivating shows complemented by insightful workshop sessions, we've proudly extended our reach to over a hundred esteemed colleges across Kerala.

Hosting Premier Auto Events

We take pride in organizing exciting MTB race events, collaborating with renowned brands. These events showcase thrilling competitions and foster a vibrant community of mountain biking enthusiasts.

We offer aerial videography, rolling platforms, surveillance robots, and custom project development. Our expert team also specializes in embedded systems for robotics and CNC aluminum parts for premium RC cars.
